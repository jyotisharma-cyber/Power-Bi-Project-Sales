##  Project Overview

The Super Store Sales Dashboard is an interactive Business Intelligence solution built in Power BI to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer segments, shipping trends, and regional sales distribution.

This dashboard helps business stakeholders monitor key performance indicators (KPIs), identify profitable areas, and make data-driven decisions to improve sales performance.

---

##  Business Objectives

- Track overall sales, profit, and order quantity.
- Analyze regional sales performance.
- Monitor monthly sales and profit trends.
- Understand customer purchasing behavior.
- Evaluate shipping methods and delivery efficiency.
- Identify top-performing product categories and sub-categories.

##  Key Insights

### Sales Performance

- Generated over **1.6 Million** in sales.
- Sold more than **22,000 units**.

### Profitability

- Achieved **175K profit**.
- Strong profit growth observed during the final quarter.

### Regional Analysis

- West region contributes the highest sales.
- South region contributes the lowest sales share.

### Customer Segments

- Consumer segment generates nearly half of total sales.
- Corporate customers contribute significantly to revenue.

### Shipping Analysis

- Standard Class dominates shipment volume.
- Same Day shipping has the lowest sales contribution.

### Product Performance

- Office Supplies lead overall sales.
- Phones are the top-performing sub-category.

##  Business Recommendations

### Expand High-Performing Regions

Focus marketing efforts on West and East regions to maximize revenue.

### Improve South Region Performance

Analyze customer behavior and sales strategies in underperforming regions.

### Optimize Shipping Operations

Encourage efficient shipping methods to reduce logistics costs.

### Increase High-Margin Product Sales

Promote profitable categories such as Technology and Phones.

### Customer Retention Programs

Develop loyalty programs targeting Consumer and Corporate segments.
